"Emerald Echoes"

Description

This piece is a reflection of the artist's journey through the vibrant landscapes of the Philippines. The use of green hues symbolizes growth and renewal, while the abstract forms represent the interconnectedness of nature and humanity. The artwork invites viewers to explore their own relationship with the environment and to find beauty in the chaos of life.
