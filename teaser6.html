"Beads of Harmony"

Description

This necklace is a stunning piece of jewelry that features a beautiful combination of natural materials. The necklace is designed to be worn as a statement piece or layered with other necklaces for a more bohemian look. Each bead is carefully selected and strung together to create a unique and eye-catching design. The necklace is finished with a durable clasp that ensures it stays securely in place while you wear it.
